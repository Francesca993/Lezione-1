Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart, cramberries and creamy Brie.It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas
FEATURED IN: Fresh Cranberry Recipes That go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

    For the Focaccia:
  • 4 cups (550gr) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 14/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
      • For the Topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubed (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, tought it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups frsh or frozen cranberries

    • Method

    1. Make the focaccia dought

    In a medium sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. you don't need to actually knead the dough, tought I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour.
